# Classic-Models_Power-BI

📊 Classic Models Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of the Classic Models dataset, focusing on sales performance, profitability, and product trends.

🔍 Key Highlights
KPIs Overview: Displays Total Sales ($9.6M), Total Profit ($3.83M), and Profit Margin (~39.8%)

Sales Insights:

-Sales distribution by product line (e.g., Classic Cars, Vintage Cars, Motorcycles)

-Top-performing products by revenue

Order Analysis:

-Quantity ordered by product line and year (2003–2005)

-Year-over-year sales comparison

Detailed Breakdown:

-Tabular view of sales, quantity, and profit by product line

Interactive Filters:

-Filter by Employee, City, Country, Order Year, and Product Line

🎯 Purpose

The dashboard helps identify high-performing products, track sales trends, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.
